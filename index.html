Why this exists

I built this after finishing university with no clear idea of where I was headed. I had skills, drive, and ambition — but no framework for connecting who I am to what I should do next. Every career quiz felt shallow. Every job board felt overwhelming. I wanted something that actually understood how I think and work, not just what I'd studied. So I built it. If you're feeling lost after graduation — or just stuck at a crossroads — this is for you.
